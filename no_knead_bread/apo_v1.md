# Anova Precision Oven

## 11-27-20
80%, 2.5%, 0.5%

Amount:
```
- 400g bread flour
- 320g water
- 10g salt
- 2g yeast
```

Procedure:
```
- 17 hour room temp
- Fold
- 6 hour secondary
- Shape

- 250C, 100% steam, rear heat, no sous vide
    - 20 min
- 205C, no steam, rear heat, no sous vide
    - 10 min

- 30 min cool
```

Notes:
This is the official standard
